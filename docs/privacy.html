Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Who We Are

FloraKnight is developed by Tekkmood (Nigeria). Contact us at tekkmood@tekkmoodarts.com with any questions about this policy.

2. What Data We Collect

FloraKnight collects the minimum data necessary to function:

  • Photos you take within the app — sent to PlantNet's API for plant identification, then stored locally on your device only.
  • Scan results — plant name, species, safety category, and scan date are stored in a local database on your device.
  • Daily scan count — stored locally on your device to manage your free scan limit. Resets every day.
  • Advertising data — our ad partner (Google AdMob) may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personal account information.
  • We do not store your photos on any server. Photos are sent to PlantNet solely for identification and are not retained by us.
  • We do not sell your data to any third party.
  • We do not track your location.

4. Third-Party Services

FloraKnight uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • PlantNet API — plant species identification. plantnet.org
  • Hugging Face — disease detection and object recognition. huggingface.co/privacy
  • Google AdMob — advertising. policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Storage

All scan history and photos are stored exclusively on your device using local SQLite storage and your device's file system. Uninstalling FloraKnight permanently deletes all this data. We have no access to it.

6. Children's Privacy

FloraKnight is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Your Rights

Since all data is stored on your device, you have full control over it. You can delete your entire scan history from within the Codex tab at any time. Uninstalling the app removes all data permanently.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. The latest version will always be accessible within the app and at this URL. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

Questions or concerns? Reach us at tekkmood@tekkmoodarts.com.
